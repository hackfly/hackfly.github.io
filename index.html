BigSeconds

A clean, minimalist clock that you can clearly see down to the second from across the room, and timers with voice alerts that countdown to your important moments.

BigSeconds - When you need a timer that can give you voice updates as it approaches your target, so that you don't just get a beep too early or late for your time-sensitive goal, but can instead be aware and ready exactly on-time. Or maybe you want gentle haptic taps or tones as your target approaches, or visual cues only - it's under your control. Featuring a second-hand that doesn't amble along its tiny circular track, but instead SNAPS forward toward each new second energetically, reflecting your own ambition and sense of purpose. This second-hand stretches half-way across your screen, and has a RACING STRIPE! BigSeconds - it's your time, use it well!

RunIntervals

Just pick your interval durations
and click GO!

Just pick your interval durations and click GO! "Plays well with others," so if you have favorite apps for audio or fitness tracking it will work well alongside those. Audio voice alerts or tones will momentarily lower the volume of your music or briefly pause spoken audio just long enough to hear the alert, then let you get right back to listening! Privacy Policy: This app does not collect, store, or track any information about you. There is a link in the app to provide feedback via email, and if used I will only use your email address to respond to your feedback.
